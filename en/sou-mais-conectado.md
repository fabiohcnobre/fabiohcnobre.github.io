    <h2>About the Project</h2>
    <p>
      The <strong>Sou + Conectado</strong> extension project is an initiative from PUC Minas Campus Poços de Caldas that aims to bring notebook technology to the community and promote digital and social inclusion.
    </p>
    <p>
      This project seeks to democratize access to technology and provide learning and development opportunities for the Poços de Caldas community, contributing to reducing digital inequality.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="user-details" id="goals">
    <h2>Project Goals</h2>
    <ul style="text-align: left; max-width: 800px; margin: 0 auto;">
      <li>Promote digital inclusion through access to notebooks and technology</li>
      <li>Offer training and education in information technology</li>
      <li>Develop digital skills in the local community</li>
      <li>Contribute to the educational and professional development of participants</li>
      <li>Create opportunities for social development through technology</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="user-details" id="impact">
    <h2>Social Impact</h2>
    <p>
      The <strong>Sou + Conectado</strong> project represents an important social commitment from PUC Minas to the Poços de Caldas community. Through this university extension initiative, students and professors work together to:
    </p>
    <ul style="text-align: left; max-width: 800px; margin: 0 auto;">
      <li>Reduce digital exclusion in underserved communities</li>
      <li>Provide equal access to technology and education</li>
      <li>Develop citizens better prepared for the digital job market</li>
      <li>Strengthen ties between the university and the community</li>
      <li>Create a collaborative and inclusive learning environment</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="user-details" id="technologies">
    <h2>Technologies Used</h2>
    <p>
      The project uses various technologies to manage and distribute resources, including:
    </p>
    <ul style="text-align: left; max-width: 800px; margin: 0 auto;">
      <li><strong>Java Virtual Machine:</strong> Platform for application development and execution</li>
      <li><strong>Database:</strong> Data management system for participants and resources</li>
      <li><strong>Management Systems:</strong> Tools for project control and monitoring</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
